Nathan Cashman

Hello, my name is Nathan Cashman and I’m a software developer transitioning into the field from a career as music educator. I graduated from Drake University with a degree in music education and spent some time working in the field before realizing education wasn’t my passion. I am passionate about learning concepts and practices integral to creating helpful and innovative software. My most recent successes have been through the DeltaV Code School in Cedar Rapids, where I worked with others to create interactive websites. In the groups I’ve been a part of, I contributed to the success of each project by identifying project requirements and risks, delegating responsibilities, debugging complex code, and overall keeping communication strong and effective within the group.
